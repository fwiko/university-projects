Attending Mentoring Sessions

Mentoring sessions have been put in place at Nottingham Trent University to provide support to first year groups. These sessions happen on a weekly basis and last for around 1 hour. Students are able to add each session they attend to their CPD

The sessions i attended happened once per week on a wednesday and were run by two 2nd year students.

Mentoring Content

The mentoring sessions covered many different topics, all of which were structured around things we needed to know or may like to know as a first-year student. In addition to support with planning, retaining knowledge, and being punctual in both attendance and when handing in work. Some of the methods used include:

  • Using checklists to plan work
  • Giving a higher priority to lesser known topics
  • Taking Rest/Not overworking
  • Which content to include in assignments
  • How/what to revise for exams
